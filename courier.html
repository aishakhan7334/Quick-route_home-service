FAQ's

We handle a wide range of items, including documents, parcels, packages, and even fragile or sensitive materials. However, there are restrictions on hazardous materials and certain oversized items.

Delivery times vary depending on the destination and service chosen. We offer options for express delivery (typically within 1-2 days) and standard delivery (3-5 days for domestic, longer for international).

While rare, if your package is lost or damaged during transit, we have a claims process in place. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible so we can assist you in filing a claim and resolving the issue promptly.

Shipping costs are based on factors such as the size and weight of the package, the shipping destination, and the selected service level (express or standard). You can obtain a quote online or through our customer service for accurate pricing.

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and electronic fund transfers. Payment options may vary depending on the region and specific service.
