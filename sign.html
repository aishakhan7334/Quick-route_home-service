FAQ's

What services are available on your platform?

Our platform offers a variety of services including electricians, mechanics, and plumbers. We also sell home essentials and tools to help you maintain and repair your home.

How do I book a service provider?

To book a service provider, simply search for the service you need, select a provider based on their ratings and availability, and book an appointment directly through our platform.

What home essentials and tools do you sell?

We offer a wide range of home essentials and tools, including electrical supplies, plumbing tools, automotive parts, and general home maintenance items.

How can I contact my service provider?

Once your booking is confirmed, you can contact your service provider directly through our messaging system.
